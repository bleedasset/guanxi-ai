# Guanxi.AI 🇨🇳

An AI-powered simulator for mastering Chinese business culture through realistic conversation practice.

## What is it?

Guanxi (关系) is the Chinese concept of relationship networks — the foundation of business in China. This app helps professionals prepare for real interactions with Chinese partners by practicing with an AI roleplay partner.

## How it works

1. You enter a business scenario (e.g. first dinner meeting with a potential partner)
2. You chat with Wei Mingzhi — an AI-powered Chinese executive
3. After each message, you receive cultural coaching feedback
4. After 4+ exchanges, get a full assessment scored across 4 dimensions:
   - **Politeness** — appropriate formality and respect
   - **Hierarchy** — awareness of rank and seniority
   - **Guanxi** — relationship-building quality
   - **Patience** — avoiding rushing to business

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend/Backend**: Next.js 14 (App Router, TypeScript)
- **AI Model**: LLaMA 3.3 70B via Groq Cloud API
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Deployment**: Vercel

## Why I built this

As someone preparing to study in China, I noticed a gap between knowing facts about Chinese culture and actually being able to navigate real business conversations. Guanxi.AI bridges that gap through practice.
